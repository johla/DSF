# DSF Glossary

## Norwegian Pension and Welfare Terms

This glossary provides English translations and explanations for Norwegian terms found throughout the DSF codebase.

## A

**AFP (Avtalefestet pensjon)**  
Early retirement pension - A contractually based pension scheme allowing retirement before the standard age (historically 67).

**AI (Arbeidsinntekt)**  
Work income - Employment earnings.

**AÅI (Årlig arbeidsinntekt)**  
Annual work income.

**Alderspensjon**  
Old age pension - Standard retirement pension (R0015 series).

**APD (Alderspensjonsgrad)**  
Old age pension degree - Percentage of pension taken (allows partial retirement).

**Arvinger**  
Heirs - Legal beneficiaries.

## B

**Barn**  
Child/children - Important for family benefits and survivor pensions.

**Barnetillegg (BT)**  
Child supplement - Additional pension for dependent children.

**Beregning**  
Calculation - Core function throughout the system.

**Bosatt**  
Resident - Residency status affects pension eligibility.

**Botid**  
Period of residence - Time lived in Norway, critical for pension calculation.

**Bruker**  
User - System user or beneficiary.

**BUP (Beregnet uførepensjon)**  
Calculated disability pension - Disability pension amount.

## D

**Dato**  
Date - Usually in format YYYYMMDD (e.g., 19670101).

**DØD**  
Deceased - Death status.

**Dødsdato**  
Date of death.

## E

**Ektefelle**  
Spouse - For survivor benefits and family considerations.

**Ektefelletillegg (ET)**  
Spouse supplement - Additional pension for supporting a spouse.

**Etterlatte**  
Survivors - Widows, widowers, and orphans.

**Etterlattepensjon**  
Survivor pension - Pension for family members of deceased (R0016 series).

**EØS (Europeisk økonomisk samarbeidsområde)**  
European Economic Area - Coordination of benefits across Europe.

## F

**FAI (Forventet årlig inntekt)**  
Expected annual income - Used for income testing.

**Familiepleier**  
Family caregiver - Person caring for family member.

**FNR (Fødselsnummer)**  
National identification number - 11-digit unique ID for every Norwegian (format: DDMMYY-XXXXX).

**Folketrygd**  
National Insurance - The Norwegian social security system.

**Folketrygdloven**  
National Insurance Act - The legislation governing the system.

**Foreldre**  
Parents.

**Forsørger**  
Provider/breadwinner - Person financially supporting others.

**Forsørgingstillegg (FT)**  
Support supplement - Additional benefit for supporting dependents.

**Framtidig trygdetid**  
Future insurance time - Projected future years of coverage.

**Fravik**  
Exemption/waiver - Exception to normal rules.

**Friinntekt**  
Income exemption - Amount of income allowed without reducing benefits.

**Frysdato**  
Freeze date - Date when certain calculations are locked.

## G

**G (Grunnbeløp)**  
Base amount - Index amount adjusted annually, basis for many calculations.

**Garanti**  
Guarantee - Guaranteed minimum benefit level.

**Garantipensjon**  
Guaranteed pension - Minimum pension level.

**Grad**  
Degree/percentage - Used for disability degree, pension percentage, etc.

**Grunnpensjon (GP)**  
Basic pension - Universal pension component (everyone qualifies with enough residence time).

## H

**Hjemmel**  
Legal basis - Reference to specific law authorizing an action.

## I

**Inntekt**  
Income - Earnings from work or other sources.

**Inntektsprøving**  
Income testing - Reduction of benefits based on other income.

## K

**Konvensjon**  
Convention/treaty - International social security agreement.

**Konvertering**  
Conversion - Converting from one pension type to another.

**Kodetillegg (KT)**  
Code supplement - Additional benefit based on special codes.

## M

**Minstepensjon**  
Minimum pension - Guaranteed minimum benefit.

**Mor**  
Mother.

## N

**NAV (Ny arbeids- og velferdsetat)**  
Norwegian Labour and Welfare Administration - Current organization.

**Netto**  
Net - Amount after deductions.

## O

**Opphør**  
Termination/cessation - End of benefits.

**Opptjening**  
Accrual/earning - Building up pension rights.

**Omsorg**  
Care - Caring for children or others.

## P

**Pensjon**  
Pension - Retirement or disability benefit.

**Pensjonsgivende inntekt (PI)**  
Pension-qualifying income - Income that counts toward pension (not all income qualifies).

**Pensjonist**  
Pensioner - Person receiving pension.

**Pensjonspoeng (PÅ)**  
Pension points - Points earned based on income, used to calculate supplementary pension.

**Poengår**  
Point year - Year in which pension points were earned.

**Pro rata**  
Proportional - Calculation method for international cases (EEA).

## R

**Reduksjon**  
Reduction - Decrease in benefits.

**Registrering**  
Registration - Data entry.

**Regulering**  
Adjustment/indexation - Annual adjustment of benefits.

## S

**Særtillegg (ST)**  
Special supplement - Extra benefit for low-income pensioners.

**Sivilstand**  
Marital status - Single, married, divorced, widowed, etc.

**Sperre**  
Block/lock - Prevents certain actions on a record.

**Sykdom**  
Illness/disease.

**Særbarn**  
Special child - Child from previous relationship.

**Søsken**  
Siblings.

## T

**Tilleggspensjon (TP)**  
Supplementary pension - Income-related pension component (based on work history).

**Tilknytning**  
Connection/relationship - Family or other relationship.

**TKNR (Trygdekontor nummer)**  
Social security office number - Local NAV office identifier.

**Trygd**  
Insurance/social security - Short for Folketrygd.

**Trygdeavgift**  
National insurance contribution - Tax funding the system.

**Trygdetid (TT)**  
Insurance time - Years of residence/coverage in Norway. Critical for pension calculation. Full pension requires 40 years.

**Trygdet**  
Insured - Covered by national insurance.

## U

**Ufør**  
Disabled - Unable to work due to illness or injury.

**Uføregrad (UFG)**  
Degree of disability - Percentage (0-100%) of reduced work capacity.

**Uførepensjon**  
Disability pension - Benefits for those unable to work (R0014 series).

**Uføretidspunkt (UFT)**  
Disability time point - Date disability began.

**Uførhet**  
Disability - State of being unable to work.

**Unntak**  
Exception - Deviation from standard rules.

**Utbetaling**  
Payment/disbursement - Actual payment of benefits.

**Utdanning**  
Education - Educational background.

**Utland**  
Foreign country - Outside Norway.

**Uttaksdato**  
Withdrawal date - Date pension payments begin.

## V

**Ventetid**  
Waiting period - Time before benefits start.

**Ventetransaksjon**  
Waiting transaction - Transaction queued for later processing.

**Verneplikt**  
Compulsory military service - Counts as insurance time.

**Vilkår**  
Condition/requirement - Eligibility criteria.

**Virkningsdato**  
Effective date - Date when decision takes effect.

## Y

**Yrke**  
Occupation/profession.

**Yrkesaktiv**  
Economically active - Working or seeking work.

**Yrkesskade**  
Occupational injury - Work-related injury or illness (R0017 series).

**Yrkesskadegrad (YUG)**  
Occupational injury degree - Percentage of disability from work injury.

**Yrkesskadetillegg (YPT)**  
Occupational injury supplement - Additional benefit for work injury.

**Yrkesskadetrygd**  
Occupational injury insurance - Coverage for work injuries.

## Ø

**ØKT**  
Increase.

**Øvrige**  
Other/miscellaneous.

## Å

**ÅFT (Årlig forsørgingstillegg)**  
Annual support supplement.

**ÅMD**  
Year-Month-Day format (YYYYMMDD).

## Technical Terms

**ACF2**  
Security system - IBM mainframe security software.

**BMS (Basic Mapping Support)**  
Screen definition system in CICS.

**Blankett**  
Form - Data entry form.

**CICS (Customer Information Control System)**  
IBM's transaction processing system.

**COMMAREA**  
Communication area - Data passed between transactions.

**DFHBMSCA**  
Standard CICS include file for screen attributes.

**DL/I**  
Database interface (older technology).

**Mapset**  
Collection of screen definitions.

**PL/I (Programming Language One)**  
IBM programming language used for DSF.

**Segment**  
Database record section - Person data divided into segments.

**TRANSID**  
Transaction identifier - 4-character code for CICS transaction.

**TSO (Time Sharing Option)**  
IBM mainframe time-sharing system.

**VSAM (Virtual Storage Access Method)**  
IBM database/file system.

## Status and Code Values

**Status Codes (examples found in code):**
- **A**: Active
- **B**: Various meanings depending on context
- **J**: Yes (Ja)
- **N**: No (Nei)
- **O**: Terminated (Opphørt)
- **S**: Blocked (Sperret)
- **X**: Various meanings depending on context

**Pension Type Codes:**
- **AP**: Alderspensjon (Old age)
- **UP**: Uførepensjon (Disability)
- **EP**: Etterlattepensjon (Survivor)
- **YP**: Yrkesskadepensjon (Occupational injury)

**Relationship Codes (TILKNYTNINGSKODE):**
- Parent, child, spouse, sibling, etc.

## Data Structure Terms

**BASED**  
PL/I keyword - Structure allocated dynamically, not statically.

**UNALIGNED**  
PL/I keyword - Compact storage, no padding between fields.

**FIXED DEC(n)**  
Fixed-point decimal number with n digits.

**CHAR(n)**  
Character string of length n.

**AREA**  
Generic storage area.

**POINTER**  
Memory address reference.

## Common Abbreviations in Code

**OMR** - Område (Area/region)  
**PTR** - Pointer  
**PEKER** - Pointer (Norwegian)  
**NR** - Nummer (Number)  
**KR** - Kroner (Norwegian currency)  
**MND** - Måned (Month)  
**ÅR** - År (Year)  
**DATO** - Date  
**KODE** - Code  
**BELØP** - Amount  
**ANT** - Antall (Count/number)

## Historical References

**1967**  
Year Norwegian National Insurance Act came into effect.

**1991**  
Major pension reform year - changed many calculations.

**P67** or **§67**  
Reference to Folketrygdloven chapter/paragraph.

**Pre-1991** / **Post-1991**  
System handles both old and new calculation rules.

**2018**  
Year DSF was decommissioned.
